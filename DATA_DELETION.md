# CredLock - Account and Data Deletion Guide

## How to Delete Your CredLock Account and Data

If you wish to delete your CredLock account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

---

## Step 1: Delete Your Vault Data (In-App)

1. Open the **CredLock** app on your device
2. Navigate to the **Vault** tab
3. Delete individual password entries by swiping or tapping the delete option on each entry
4. Alternatively, go to **Settings** → **Sign Out** to disconnect your account

---

## Step 2: Delete Google Drive Backups (If Any)

If you have created backups to Google Drive:

### Option A: Delete via CredLock App
1. Open **CredLock**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Restore from Google Drive**
4. View the list of backups
5. Long-press or select each backup and delete it

### Option B: Delete via Google Drive
1. Go to [Google Drive Settings](https://drive.google.com/drive/settings)
2. Click **Manage Apps**
3. Find **CredLock** in the list
4. Click **Options** → **Delete hidden app data**
5. Confirm deletion

---

## Step 3: Uninstall the App

1. On your Android device, go to **Settings** → **Apps**
2. Find and select **CredLock**
3. Tap **Uninstall**
4. Confirm uninstallation

This will remove all local data stored on your device.

---

## Step 4: Revoke Google Sign-In Access (Optional)

To completely revoke CredLock's access to your Google account:

1. Go to your [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Find **CredLock** in the list of connected apps
3. Click on it and select **Remove Access**

---

## What Data is Deleted

When you complete the steps above, the following data is deleted:

### ✅ Deleted Immediately
- **Vault passwords, usernames, PINs, and notes** stored on your device (deleted when you uninstall the app)
- **App settings and preferences** (deleted when you uninstall the app)
- **Encryption keys** stored in device secure storage (deleted when you uninstall the app)
- **Google Drive backups** (deleted when you manually remove them from Google Drive)

### ⚠️ What We Retain
- **Nothing** - CredLock does not store any of your data on our servers. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive.

---

## Data Retention Period

- **Local device data:** Deleted immediately upon app uninstallation
- **Google Drive backups:** Remain until you manually delete them (see Step 2)
- **Google account information:** Access is revoked immediately when you disconnect (see Step 4)
